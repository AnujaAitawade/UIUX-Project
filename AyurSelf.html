Ayurvedic Circadian Rhythm

The world's energy shifts every 4 hours. Your body should too.

2 AM - 6 AM

Vata Time

Ether & Air. The "Ambrosial Hours." Best for meditation, prayer, and subtle learning.

6 AM - 10 AM

Kapha Time

Earth & Water. Body feels heavy. Use this time for physical exercise to wake up the system.

10 AM - 2 PM

Pitta Time

Fire & Water. Digestive fire (Agni) is highest. Eat your largest meal now.

2 PM - 6 PM

Vata Time

Lightness returns. Ideal for communication, creative work, and social interaction.

6 PM - 10 PM

Kapha Time

Stability. Eat a light dinner. Relax the mind. Be in bed by 10 PM for deep healing.

10 PM - 2 AM

Pitta Time

Internal fire cleanses your organs. If you are awake, you might get "midnight munchies." Sleep through this!
